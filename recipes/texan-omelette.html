Texan Omelette

Description

Indulge in our hearty Southern-Style Omelette, filled with fluffy eggs, smoky ham, and sharp cheddar cheese. Sautéed bell peppers and onions add a pop of flavor, while a hint of cayenne gives it a subtle kick. This combination can be topped with fresh diced tomatoes and a sprinkle of chives to level up its overall taste and appearance.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup choice of bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup of diced onion(preferably yellow)
  • 1/4 cup sliced ham
  • 1/3 cup of shredded cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • cayenne (adjust to taste)
  • tomatoes (garnish)
  • chives (garnish)
  • 1 Tb of cooking oil or 1/4 butter

Steps

  1. Prep Ingredients: Gather and chop all your ingredients: dice the ham, bell pepper, onion, and tomatoes(optional). Shred the cheddar cheese if not pre-shredded.
  2. Whisk Eggs:In a bowl, crack the eggs and whisk them until well combined. Season with a pinch of salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper.
  3. Sauté Vegetables:In a non-stick skillet, heat the butter or oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and bell pepper. Sauté for about 3-4 minutes until they are softened.
  4. Add Ham:Stir in the diced smoky ham and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Pour Eggs:Pour the whisked eggs over it. Let it cook undisturbed for about 2-3 minutes, or until the edges start to set; Another indicator for a ready bedding is when the bubbles become more uniform.
  6. Add Cheese:A little before the eggs begin to settle in the skillet evenly distribute the shredded cheese.
  7. Add Sautéd VegetablesOn one half of the omelette reight after the cheese has been added to the omelette.
  8. Fold Omelette:Carefully fold the omelette in half over the cheese using a spatula. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese melts and the eggs are fully cooked.
  9. Plate and Garnish:Slide the omelette onto a plate. Garnish with fresh diced tomatoes and chopped chives.
